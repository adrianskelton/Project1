Mountain magic 1 - Family unit

6 guests | 2 bedrooms | 4 beds | 1.5 baths

Our newly renovated family unit container home consists of two containers panoramic views of the lakes, ocean and mountains. There is a bbq or as we call it in South Africa a braai and seating between the two containers as well as an outside bath if you wish to take in the view while relaxing in some bubbles. The unit features two containers one with a double bed and the other with two bunk beds, so you can enjoy the feeling of being on holiday together though having a bit of privacy from the kids. Included in our family container price is a 50% off voucher on tandem paragliding session by fly for africa, the most popular and trusted paragliding company in the western cape. Their launch area is on our property! Though don't worry its quite a bit away from your container so you won't be disturbed by them.
